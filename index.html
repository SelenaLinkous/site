The Studio

Bridging Vision and Structure

Based in the heart of Portland, Oregon, Liminal Architecture LLC is a boutique firm dedicated to professional, scientific, and technical services within the architectural sector. Our practice, steered by Selena Linkous, focuses on the "liminal" space where human experience meets physical form.

With a robust background in architectural engineering and related services, we maintain a standard of excellence reflected in our significant annual revenue and industry positioning. We believe every project is an opportunity to redefine the Pacific Northwest skyline.

Our commitment to precision ensures that every site-specific detail is handled with scientific rigor and creative flair.
